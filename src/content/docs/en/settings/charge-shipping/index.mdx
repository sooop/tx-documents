To pay for shipping on SmartShip, you need to top up your TxMoney balance first.

<Callout type="info">
### What is TxMoney?

- TxMoney is the currency used to pay for shipping fees and other charges incurred while using SmartShip.
- When a shipment arrives at the center, the shipping fee is calculated and automatically deducted from your TxMoney account.
- Subsequent charges after dispatch (fulfillment surcharges, import duties billed to sender, etc.) are also automatically deducted from TxMoney at the time of billing.
- If your TxMoney balance is insufficient when a shipment arrives, the inbound process will fail and processing will be delayed. Please keep a sufficient balance at all times.
</Callout>

### Menu Location

Cost Management >> TxMoney Top-up

<AnnotatedImage src="topup-txmoney-page" section="settings" alt="TxMoney top-up page" caption="Cost Management > TxMoney Top-up screen" />

1. **My TxMoney Account** — View your current balance and any outstanding unpaid charges.
2. **Enter Top-up Amount** — Type an amount directly or use the quick-add buttons to select a preset amount.
3. **Bank Transfer** — Receive a virtual account number and transfer funds.
4. **Paynow / Credit or Debit Card** — Pay instantly by card.

### How to Top Up

1. Select or enter the amount you wish to top up.
2. Choose a payment method.

* Bank Transfer (Virtual Account)
    - Click the **[Bank Transfer]** button
    - Transfer to the displayed virtual account number (account holder: **Tracx\_CompanyName**, e.g. Tracx\_SmithTrading)
    - Top-up is completed automatically after transfer is confirmed
      Virtual accounts are valid for 7 days. If expired, you must request a new virtual account.
* Kookmin · BC · Woori · Lotte Card (no fee) — Korea domestic payment
    1. Click the **[Kookmin · BC · Woori · Lotte Card]** button
    2. Agree to terms and select your card issuer
    3. Click **[Pay]** to proceed
    4. Top-up is completed automatically after payment
* All Cards (international payment)
    1. Click the **[All Cards]** button
    2. Check the notice and click **Pay**
    3. Enter your card number, expiry date, and CVC
    4. Enter the cardholder name
    5. Click **[Pay]**
    6. Top-up is completed automatically after payment

<Callout type="warning">
### Important Notes on Card Payment

- **[All Cards]** uses Stripe, a payment gateway based in Singapore. Your card statement will show the approval country as **SINGAPORE**.
- Because a Singapore PG is used, the payment is processed as an overseas KRW transaction.
- If your card issuer has an **overseas KRW payment block** enabled, the payment may not go through.
- If the currency charged is not SGD, your card issuer may apply **foreign transaction fees** according to their policy.
</Callout>

## Balance & Usage Notes

- For seller-dispatched shipments, the shipping fee is automatically calculated and deducted from your TxMoney balance when the shipment arrives at the center.
- Once TxMoney has been partially used after a top-up, refunds are not available for the remaining balance.
- If you withdraw from SmartShip, you may receive a refund of your remaining balance minus a 5% fee. (Refunds are processed on the 15th of the month following your withdrawal request.)
- If a payment was completed but TxMoney was not credited, or if an incorrect amount was charged, you may request a cancellation before the balance is used. Please contact support:
   - Korea merchants: help-kr@tracxlogis.com or live chat
   - Japan merchants: help-jp@tracxlogis.com or live chat
   - Singapore merchants: help-sg@tracxlogis.com
